My Story

Charles Jerewin M. Meer

I grew up in Brgy. Stabe, Canlubang, Calamba City, with my family — a fairly quiet upbringing that taught me the value of steady routines. Today, I live in an apartment near City College of Calamba, where I'm pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

Being a Computer Science student means juggling deadlines, quizzes, exams, and long-term projects, often all at once. There's no way through that without discipline and planning — so over time, I built systems for myself instead of relying on memory or good intentions.

I use Google Calendar to track every class, deadline, quiz, online session, and project, so nothing catches me off guard. I also keep my Gmail inbox organized with labels, which means important messages stay visible while newsletters and notifications get filed away without cluttering my day.

Somewhere along the way, I realized this wasn't just a coping mechanism for school — I genuinely enjoy organizing digital workspaces and helping things run more smoothly. That realization is what led me here.

Why I Chose Administrative Virtual Assistance

Every hour a business owner spends sorting emails, resolving calendar conflicts, or updating records is an hour that could go toward growing their business instead. I want to be the person who takes those tasks off their plate.

What draws me to this work isn't just completing tasks — it's building organized systems that make everyday operations easier to manage. My Computer Science background shapes how I approach that: logically, methodically, and with an eye for where a process can be improved. I don't just want to manage an inbox or copy over data — I want to notice where a workflow is clunky and quietly make it better.

Right now, my focus is simple: learn how a client's workflow actually runs — their inbox, their calendar, their day-to-day — and become someone they can hand things to without a second thought. If I do that well, it's not just another task checked off. It's an extra hour back in someone's day.
